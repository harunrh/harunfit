My Journey

In 2019, I stepped into the gym as a skinny teenager who just wanted to feel comfortable in his own skin. I had no idea what I was doing, no structured training, no understanding of nutrition, just a strong desire to change.

Over the years, I not only gained the knowledge of training and nutrition but also developed confidence, a strong mindset, and a deeper understanding of what it truly takes, not just to build a body you're proud of, but to establish lasting habits and principles that go beyond fitness.

My next mission is to help others who are in the same position I once was. If you're ready to make a change, I'm here to guide you through the process.
